Welcome to Maharashtra

Capital: Mumbai

Established: 1 May 1960

Area: 307,713 km²

Population: ~124 million (as per 2021 estimates)

Languages Spoken: Marathi, Hindi, English

Famous for: Bollywood, Gateway of India, Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Lavasa, and Pune’s educational institutions.

Climate: Coastal regions have a tropical climate, while interior regions have hot summers and mild winters.

Best Time to Visit: October to March for pleasant weather.

Festivals: Ganesh Chaturthi, Gudi Padwa, Makar Sankranti, Diwali
