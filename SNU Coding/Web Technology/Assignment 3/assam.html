Welcome to Assam

Capital: Dispur

Established: 26 January 1950

Area: 78,438 km²

Population: ~35 million (as per 2021 estimates)

Languages Spoken: Assamese, Bengali, Hindi, Bodo

Famous for: Kaziranga National Park, Assam Tea, Majuli (world’s largest river island), and Bihu dance.

Climate: Subtropical with hot summers, heavy monsoon rains, and mild winters.

Best Time to Visit: October to April for comfortable weather.

Festivals: Bihu, Durga Puja, Ambubachi Mela, Jonbeel Mela
