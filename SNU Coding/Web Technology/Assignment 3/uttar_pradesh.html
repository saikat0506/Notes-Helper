Welcome to Uttar Pradesh

Capital: Lucknow

Established: 26 January 1950

Area: 243,286 km²

Population: ~231 million (as per 2021 estimates)

Languages Spoken: Hindi (official), Urdu, English

Famous for: Taj Mahal, Varanasi Ghats, Kumbh Mela, Ayodhya, Lucknow’s Nawabi heritage, and delicious street food.

Climate: Varied climate with hot summers, monsoons, and cold winters.

Best Time to Visit: October to March for sightseeing and comfortable weather.

Festivals: Diwali, Holi, Navratri, Kumbh Mela
